Receiver Sensitivity

How good your router can "hear".

Depends on modulation and bit rate and your router hardware.

Receiver Sensitivity


Example: Ubiquiti Bullet M RX Sensitivity

Signal to Noise Ratio


  • EM noise is usually at around -90dB to -110dB
    (but only displayed by some drivers)
  • Signal Strength - Noise Level = Signal Margin
  • > 40dB: excellent signal
  • < 10dB: weak link

Example:
(-40dBm) - (-100dBm) = 60dB Signal Margin

Why does my device get disconnected with 20dB signal margin?

  • Good signal margins required on both devices
  • Signal level shows only what's being received
